About us
Quan organization is a non-profit organization that has a goal to reduce the risk of cybercrime in our society by encouraging the online population to take proactive steps against it through the sharing of information, as well as by guiding and supporting them as they develop peaceful solutions to the concerns and challenges it raises. Social media has become a powerful tool for sharing information and knowledge. Facebook, Instagram, Youtube, TikTok, and Twitter are the most commonly used social media platforms. Since the target audience for this advocacy are those who are regularly online, our anti-cybercrime advocacy will be promoted by posting, sharing publicity materials, and advocacy messages through social media platforms.
